ADMINISTRATION

Kasese district has an administrative hierachy as follows:

District Chair person: who takes overall control of the disrict.

Chief Administrative Officer: who oversees all civil servants in the district and makes sure that all workers in the district are doing their roles and cared for appropriately.

Human Resource: who is responsible of all deployments made in the district. He also makes sure that all workers are paid on time.

Sub-county Chairpersons: take care of Sub-counties as small administrative units in the district.

Councillors: these represent sub-counties at the district council.
